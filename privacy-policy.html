Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

habby is committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our users. We believe in minimalist design and minimal data collection. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

habby collects only essential technical information needed to maintain app functionality and stability:

  • Error logs and crash reports via Sentry
  • Basic app performance metrics

We explicitly DO NOT collect:

  • Personal journal entries
  • User identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device information beyond what is automatically included in crash reports
  • Usage patterns or analytics
  • Any other personal information

How We Use Information

The limited technical information we collect is used solely for:

  • Identifying and fixing app errors and crashes
  • Maintaining app stability
  • Improving app performance

Data Storage and Security

  • Error logs are stored securely through Sentry's platform
  • No user content is stored on our servers
  • All journal entries remain locally on your device

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is:

  • Sentry: For error tracking and crash reporting

Data Retention

Error logs and crash reports are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Your Rights

While we don't collect personal information, you have the right to:

  • Request information about any technical data associated with your app instance
  • Request deletion of any error logs associated with your app instance
  • Opt out of error reporting entirely through app settings

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed. Users will be notified of any material changes through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [your contact information].

Additional Information

habby is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in minimalism not just in design, but also in data collection. Our goal is to provide a simple, private journaling experience where your thoughts remain yours alone.
